Phase Details
Florian
Java Solo
Aug 1 – 7
Arrive Jakarta, spend max 1–2 days — it's chaotic and not very tourist-friendly. Hop on the Argo Lawu night train to Yogyakarta. Spend 5 days exploring Borobudur at sunrise, Prambanan, the Kraton palace, and Malioboro street food. Day trip to Mount Merapi volcano.
Book train in advance · KAI app Borobudur sunrise ticket Hostel ~€6–10/night Warung food ~€1–2/meal
Florian Friend 2
East Java & Bromo
Aug 8 – 16
Friend 2 flies into Jakarta Aug 8 — either meet in Yogya (they fly direct) or briefly back to Jakarta. Head east toward Mount Bromo via Probolinggo — go independently, not via overland tour, saves a lot. Bromo sunrise is one of Indonesia's most iconic experiences. Then cross to Bali by ferry from Banyuwangi → Gilimanuk — an adventure in itself and very cheap.
Skip Bromo tour operators Ferry: ~€3 Banyuwangi→Gili Citilink/Lion Air for flights Jeep hire at Bromo: split 4-way

Bali — All Three
Aug 17 – 23
Friend 1 flies into Denpasar (DPS) — best airport given you're already in Bali. Slow down here: Ubud for rice terraces, cooking classes, and monkey forest. Uluwatu for the clifftop temple and Kecak fire dance at sunset. Canggu for the beach and social scene. Bali rewards not over-scheduling it.
Ubud cooking class ~€25 Scooter hire ~€4/day Book Ubud early — peak season Uluwatu sunset: unmissable
Florian Friend 2 Friend 1
Lombok & Gili Islands
Aug 24 – 29
Fast boat from Bali to Gili T (~2h, ~€15–20). Gili Trawangan for the social scene, Gili Meno for quiet and snorkeling. Consider the Mount Rinjani trek (2–3 days, one of Indonesia's great hikes — book guides in advance). End in Lombok, fly DPS from Lombok airport or short ferry back.
Fast boat: book in advance Rinjani guides: book weeks ahead Snorkel gear rental ~€3/day No cars on Gili — bikes only
Route Overview
⚠ Friend 1 — Flight Not Booked
Recommend flying into Denpasar (DPS) on Aug 17 — you'll already be in Bali. Jakarta doesn't make sense given only 12 days remain. Check Citilink, Lion Air, AirAsia for cheap options. Book soon — Aug 17 is peak season.
Essential Tips
🚂
KAI app — book the Jakarta→Yogya night train well in advance. Fills up fast in August.
💸
Carry IDR cash. ATMs in smaller areas run out or charge high fees. Withdraw in cities.
🌋
Rinjani guides must be booked weeks in advance. Don't leave this last minute.
🏠
Book Ubud accommodation now. August is peak season — good budget spots sell out.
✈
Citilink & Lion Air for domestic flights — very cheap. Check Skyscanner regularly.
🛡️
Travel insurance is non-negotiable. Bromo and Rinjani involve real altitude and risk.
🍜
Eat at warungs (local family spots) — nasi goreng and bakso for €1–2. Skip tourist restaurants.
🤿
The Gili Islands snorkeling is exceptional — turtles are almost guaranteed around Gili Meno.
