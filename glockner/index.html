01

Friday, June 6  ·  Arrive 3pm

The Lake & the Town

3:00 pm
Check in — Pension Buchner ~€45/night

Central guesthouse, 2 min walk to the lake. Clean, well-reviewed, no fuss. Book in advance — fills up on race weekend.

4:00 pm
Explore the lake Free or ~€12
Option A — Lake Boat Tour (~€12): 1h scenic cruise from the Esplanade pier. Open-deck boat, mountain panorama all around, departs every hour until ~5pm.
Option B — Walk the lake (free): The full loop is ~13km but you can do half and turn back. Best free thing in town — spectacular evening light on the water with the Schmittenhöhe rising behind.
5:30 pm
Old Town Stroll Free

Walk the Elisabeth-Promenade and through Stadtplatz. Medieval Vogtturm tower, swans on the lake, local shops. No entry needed — just walk.

6:30 pm
Swim — Strandbad Zell am See Free

Crystal-clear, drinking-water-quality lake. Diving platforms, grass for sitting. Open until 7pm. Mountain backdrop makes this one of the best free swims in Austria.

8:00 pm
Dinner — Christian's Gasthaus ~€12–15

Honest Austrian Gasthaus, enormous portions, Schnitzel around €12. Not on the tourist strip. Open Fri–Sun from 2pm.

02

Saturday, June 7  ·  Full day out

Gorge, Glacier & Bib Pickup

7:15 am
Breakfast — HOFER supermarket ~€5

Bread, cheese, cold cuts, fruit — and a packed lunch for the whole day. This is the move for all three days; avoid the lakefront cafés.

8:30 am
Bus 660 to Kaprun ~€5

From Zell am See Postplatz. ~20 min to Kaprun Ortsmitte. Check the summer timetable on oebb.at the night before.

9:15 am
Sigmund-Thun-Klamm gorge ~€5

10 min walk from the bus stop. Wooden walkways bolted directly into the cliff face above a roaring torrent. About 1 hour, open from 9am. Suitable for all fitness levels — but the noise and drama is unlike anything else.

For you (race eve): Very gentle walking, legs stay fresh.

Alternative morning: Bus 71 to the Schmittenhöhe cable car (~€20 return) — ride up to 1,965m and walk the panorama trail above Zell am See. Better if you prefer open mountain views over enclosed gorge drama.
11:00 am
Kitzsteinhorn — Gipfelwelt 3000 ~€40 cable car

Bus 660 continues from Kaprun to the valley station. Cable cars to 3,029m — real glacier, 360° Alps panorama. Eat your packed lunch at the top. Last cable car down ~4:45pm.

Budget alternative (save ~€40): Walk or take the bus to the Kaprun reservoir (Stausee) — a free alpine walk to a dramatic high-mountain dam. Great scenery, zero cost.
3:30 pm
Train to Bruck — Expo & Bib Pickup €1.50

YOU Collect your bib at the race office.
FRIENDS Go to the Merchandisezelt next to the race office for Besucherbus tickets.

⚠️ Critical: Only 244 Besucherbus tickets. €25/person. On sale Sat 10:00–19:00. Don't leave it past 5pm.
7:30 pm
Dinner — pasta carb-load €0–15

Pension kitchen + Hofer pasta = €3. Otherwise Christian's Gasthaus. Train back from Bruck is 5 min. Early night — race starts at 7am.

03

Sunday, June 8  ·  Race Day

The Glocknerkönig

5:45 am
Early train to Bruck €1.50

Everyone takes the first ÖBB train from Zell am See to Bruck-Fusch. 5 min. Check first Sunday morning departure on oebb.at — aim to be in Bruck by 6:10am.

6:15 am
FRIENDS Board Besucherbus at Maximarkt

Bus departs Maximarkt Bruck 6:15am → Bahnhofstraße 6:30–6:40 → Fusch 6:45 → Ferleiten 6:55. Direct to finish area at Fuscher Törl (2,428m). Return from 10:30am, last bus 12:00pm.

7:00 am
YOU Race start — Dorfplatz Bruck

27.3 km  ·  1,672 vertical metres  ·  max 12%
Bruck (756m) → Fusch → Ferleiten (1,145m) → Fuscher Törl (2,428m).
Refreshment at Piffkar restaurant, km 19.2. Sprint classification Bruck → Ferleiten.

~8:30–9:30 am
FRIENDS Cheer you across the finish at 2,428m

Friends are already at the top when you arrive. Café at Fuscher Törl for a hot drink. First bus back down at 10:30am.

After 10:30 am
Sunday afternoon
Option A — Kaiser-Franz-Josefs-Höhe hike: Once the road opens at 10:30am, hitch a ride up with race visitors to KFJ-Höhe (2,369m). Walk the Gamsgrubenweg trail above the Pasterze glacier. Marmots everywhere. Free once up there.
Option B — Lake afternoon (free): Train back to Zell am See, swim at the Strandbad. After a 5am start, sometimes a quiet lake afternoon is the right call.
2:00 pm
Celebration meal — Christian's Gasthaus ~€15

Open Sundays from 2pm. Schnitzel, Tafelspitz, Kaiserschmarrn for dessert. The right place to end the weekend.
